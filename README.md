# BAZ BROTHERS — Marketing Analytics Project

**Tools:** SQL · Excel · Python (pandas, matplotlib)  
**Period:** Jul–Dec 2025  
**Role context:** Marketing Analyst, wholesale vintage apparel

## Project Summary
Built a channel performance and customer segmentation analysis 
for a DTC brand running paid media across Meta, Google, and Amazon.

## Key Findings
- Email delivers **4.3x ROAS** vs Paid Social's 1.6x at 12% of the spend
- High-value customers (32% of base) drive 40% of total revenue
- West region leads in both revenue and avg customer LTV
- December revenue hit $4,873 — highest month, +8.8% MoM

## Analysis Includes
- ROAS by channel (paid media efficiency)
- Customer segmentation — High / Mid / Low Value
- Monthly revenue trend with MoM growth
- Ad spend vs. revenue scatter analysis
- Revenue by region and product category
- Average LTV by region

- `CASE WHEN` for customer segmentation
- `UNION ALL` for multi-platform data unification
