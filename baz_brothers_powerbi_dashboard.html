AI-Powered Insight · Key Findings
Email dominates ROI
Email delivers 4.3x ROAS vs Paid Social's 1.6x — at $2,200 vs $18,500 spend. Reallocating 20% of Paid Social budget to email nurture could unlock $3,400+ incremental revenue.
High-value customers drive outsized revenue
Top 32% of customers (LTV ≥$400) generate the majority of revenue. Mid-value cohort (45%) is the highest-leverage conversion opportunity.
West region leads, South underindexes
West contributes 30% of revenue with highest avg LTV. South shows lowest LTV — potential for targeted retention campaigns.
